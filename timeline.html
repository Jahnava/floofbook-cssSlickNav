What is A Floofbook Timeline?

Floofbook Timeline is where you share your favorite pictures, posts, and experiences with your floof-friends on Floofbook. Floofbook Timeline allows you to:

  • Add your favorite photos.
  • View other floofs newest photos.
  • Edit your personal information.
  • View your activity on Floofbook.
  • Update your Floofbook status.
  • Share public posts from other floofs.
  • Tell your floof-friends about your day.
  • Share your videos or other videos that you like.
  • Post quizzes and games to do with your floof-friends.

The Floofbook Timeline is a Great Place to Express Yourself!
