Guide for Humans

Understanding your Dog

We strive to create understanding and bridge the communication gap between owners and their dog friends. Dogs are able to understand and write most human words, but there are a few words that humans may not understand while navigating around the site. I hope this guide helps!

What is a floof?
Floof is another way of saying dog.
What are barks?
The word "Barks" is referring to a dog's use of language. Barking is how dogs speak.
What does it mean when a dog writes pawsome?
Pawsome is a playful way of saying awesome, but dogs like to include the word paw for fun. This word is also common in cat communities.
What is a hooman?
Hooman is the word that dogs use to refer to their human.
What is a wag?
When you see the word "wag" it's referring to a dog wagging their tail. When a dog wags their tail it means they're happy.
